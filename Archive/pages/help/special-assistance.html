SPECIAL ASSISTANCE

SkyVoyage is committed to making air travel accessible for everyone. Request assistance at least 48 hours before your flight.

WHEELCHAIR

Gate-to-gate and curb-to-gate wheelchair service at no extra charge.

VISUAL IMPAIRMENT

Dedicated crew support, braille cards, and audio announcements.

HEARING IMPAIRMENT

Visual alerts, sign language trained crew on select routes.

MEDICAL SUPPORT

Oxygen, stretcher facilities, and medical equipment clearance.

CONTACT ACCESSIBILITY TEAM

Our dedicated accessibility helpline is available 24/7
