PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 • Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, passport details, travel preferences, and payment information solely for the purpose of facilitating flight bookings and providing you with a personalized travel experience.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • ✓ Processing flight bookings and payments
  • ✓ Sending booking confirmations and travel updates
  • ✓ Improving our services and user experience
  • ✓ Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • ✓ Fraud prevention and security measures

3. DATA PROTECTION

We employ industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption, PCI-DSS compliant payment processing, and regular security audits to protect your personal data. Your information is stored on secure servers with restricted access controls.

4. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@skyvoyage.in.
