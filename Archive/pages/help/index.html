FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I correct a name spelling?

Minor spelling corrections of up to 3 characters can be done via our Support Desk. Major name changes require flight cancellation and rebooking. Contact us at support@skyvoyage.in for assistance.

What is the refund processing time?

Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation. Credits will be sent back to the original payment method. For urgent refunds, contact our premium helpline.

Can I carry musical instruments on board?

Small instruments (guitar size and below) can be carried in the cabin if they fit in the overhead bin. Larger instruments may require a purchased seat. Contact us for specific instrument clearance.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

For domestic flights, arrive at least 2 hours before departure. For international flights, arrive at least 3 hours prior. Premium passengers with SkyPriority enjoy expedited processing.
